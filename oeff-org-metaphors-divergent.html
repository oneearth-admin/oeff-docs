OEFF 2026 — Structure Explorations

Five metaphors for the same team. Which one feels right?

Concentric Orbits

Solar

Core decisions radiate outward. Proximity to center = proximity to decisions, not rank. Everyone owns a direction.

Says: "We orbit shared purpose. No one is above — some are closer to the center of gravity." 1 / 5

Underground Network

Mycelium

The visible work (fruiting bodies) is supported by invisible connections underneath. No center — just density of connection. Nutrients flow where they're needed.

Says: "We're connected underground. What you see is the fruit — the real work is the network beneath." 3 / 5
Re-entanglement

Weave

Warp threads (domains) cross weft threads (people). Where they intersect, work happens. No single thread holds the fabric — it's the crossing that creates strength.

Says: "Every thread matters. The fabric is the intersections — where your work crosses someone else's domain." 4 / 5
River System

Watershed

Each person is a tributary. Their work flows downstream toward the festival. Where streams meet, coordination happens. The festival is the ocean everything flows into.

Says: "Everything flows toward the festival. Your stream is yours — but it joins the river." 5 / 5
