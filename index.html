Subject Prefixes

  • [Action by DATE] — Need them to do something
  • [Q:] — Asking a question
  • [FYI] — No action needed
  • [Confirmed] — Closing the loop

Key Habits

  • Always reply FROM the shared address
  • Confirm receipt: "Got it — will review by Thursday"
  • Close loops: "Resolved — closing thread"
  • Point to forms, don't collect info in email
How do I switch the "From" address when replying?

Click reply, then click the From field at the top of the compose window. Select films@oneearthfilmfest.org or hosts@ from the dropdown.

What if a reply goes to my personal inbox?

Forward the thread to the shared inbox, then reply FROM the shared address. This pulls the conversation into shared space.

Where do I see all shared inbox threads?

Go to groups.google.com → click OEFF Films or OEFF Hosts → click Conversations in the sidebar. Bookmark this for quick access.

How do I hand off a thread to someone else?

No need to forward — it's already in the shared inbox. Just tag in the Team Space if it's urgent or needs specific attention.
