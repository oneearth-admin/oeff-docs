Kim Kickoff Meeting

30 minutes — Garen + Ana + Kim — Week of Feb 17

Meeting Purpose

Get Kim oriented and aligned on her first project. She should leave this call knowing exactly what to do, who to ask, and what "done" looks like.

Agenda

TimeTopic
0:00–5:00 Intros and context
How Kim got connected to OEFF. What excites her about this. Quick round of who does what.
5:00–15:00 OEFF in plain language (Ana leads)
  • What the festival is: community venues screening environmental films across Chicagoland
  • What this year looks like: ~15+ venues, April 22–28, distributed model with contractors
  • Where communication breaks down: multiple channels, unclear routing, everything funneling through 2–3 people
  • Where we are with adoption: we've been moving from manual coordination to platform-based tools (shared inboxes, host guide, webinar registration). It's working, but there have been stumbly moments — a date display issue confused some hosts, and the team is still building muscle memory around the new workflows. That's normal for year one, not dysfunction.
  • What we're hoping Kim can help with: fresh perspective on whether our docs and processes make sense to someone who doesn't already know everything
15:00–22:00 The starter project (Garen walks through)
  • What she's reviewing: host guide, team docs site, host communication templates
  • The three anchor questions: What's unclear? What's missing? What could be systematized?
  • Where the materials live: Google Drive folder + NotebookLM workspace
  • Timeline: ~6–8 hours over 10 days, deliverable is an audit summary in whatever format feels natural
  • Compensation: $40/hr, drawn from collaborator pool
22:00–27:00 Logistics
  • Primary contact: Garen for day-to-day questions
  • Check-in: quick sync after ~1 week
  • Communication: email or text, whatever Kim prefers
  • Confirm access: Google Drive shared, NotebookLM set up
27:00–30:00 Questions and close
Open floor. Anything unclear? Anything she wants to know about the team or the org before diving in?

After the Meeting
