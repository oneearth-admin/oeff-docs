# OEFF Email Groups

We have five email groups on oneearthcollective.org. Three are for reaching people on our team. Two are for communication with external partners (hosts and filmmakers).

---

## Internal Groups — Reaching the Team

Use these when you need to email a specific group of people on the OEFF planning team. Messages go to everyone in the group.

### squad@oneearthcollective.org
**The full OEFF planning team.** All-hands announcements, meeting notes, anything the whole squad should see.

**Who's in it:** Ana, Ahmad, Andy, Cesar, Erin, Garen, Jani, Joanna, Joshua, Kim, Maria, Michael, Peter, Sanaa (14 people)

### core@oneearthcollective.org
**Leadership and key coordinators.** Budget discussions, strategic decisions, planning threads that don't need 14 inboxes.

**Who's in it:** Ana, Cesar, Erin, Garen, Joshua, Kim, Michael (7 people)

### interns@oneearthcollective.org
**The intern cohort.** Scheduling, availability surveys, festival week assignments, onboarding materials.

**Who's in it:** Jani, Maria, Peter, Sanaa (4 people)

**Quick rule of thumb:**
- Need everyone? **squad@**
- Leadership or coordination thread? **core@**
- Intern-specific? **interns@**

---

## External Groups — Partner Communication

These are shared inboxes. When a host or filmmaker emails one of these addresses, the message lands in a shared inbox that the support team can see, assign, and respond to. Replies come from the group address, not your personal email.

### hosts@oneearthcollective.org
**Where screening hosts reach us.** Hosts email this address for logistics help, tech questions, and general inquiries. This is the contact address linked from the host guide.

**Support team:** Ana, Joshua, Erin, Garen, Kim

### films@oneearthcollective.org
**Where filmmakers and distributors reach us.** Format availability, file delivery, QC status, licensing questions.

**Support team:** Garen, Joshua, Ana, Erin, Peter, Sanaa

---

## Good to Know

- **Internal groups are one-way announcements** — replies go to the sender, not the whole group. If you want a group discussion, say "reply all" in your message.
- **External groups are shared inboxes** — everyone on the support team sees incoming messages and can respond. You can assign conversations and mark them resolved.
- **Everyone on interns@ is also on squad@.** You don't need to email both.
- **YFC collaborators** (Sue, Lisa, Sandy) are not on the internal groups — reach them directly at their oneearthyfc.org addresses.
