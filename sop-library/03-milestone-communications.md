# Milestone Communications — Stream 3

**Owner:** Kim (drafts and sends) / Executive Director (approves messaging changes)
**Backup:** Garen
**Tools:** Mailmeteor, Google Drive, V7 Google Sheet, hosts@ Gmail
**Audience:** Practitioner
**Reading context:** Desk-doing — check the calendar each week, prep the next milestone send

---

## What this is

Broader updates that go to all hosts at key moments in the festival cycle. These are not weekly — they fire on specific dates tied to operational milestones. Every email either tells hosts to do something specific or gives them something they'll need soon. No "just checking in" emails.

---

## The calendar

```
~Mar 9     "Your film is confirmed"     Per-host, as film matching completes
~Mar 23    Marketing kit ready           Promo materials available for download
~Apr 1     Screening packet shipped      Film file + everything hosts need
~Apr 15    One week out                  Final logistics, slides, emergency contacts
~Apr 21    Eve of festival               Short, warm. "We're excited. Call this number."
~Apr 29    Thank you + debrief survey    Gratitude first, then survey link
```

## Step by step

### 1. "Your film is confirmed" — ~Mar 9 (or as matching completes)

**Template:** Film confirmation email (to be drafted)
**Merge fields:** {{First Name}}, {{Film Title}}, {{Venue Name}}, {{Screening Date}}, {{Film Synopsis}}, {{Trailer Link}}

**What to do:**
1. Wait for film matching to be complete — Executive Director or Operations confirms
2. Verify each host's film assignment in V7 Sheet
3. Draft email in Mailmeteor. Include: film title, brief synopsis, trailer link
4. Send test. Check that per-host fields populated correctly
5. Send to warm host list (batch by screening date if staggered)

**Decision rights:** You own the send once film assignments are confirmed. Don't send until Operations confirms matching is final.

---

### 2. Marketing kit ready — ~Mar 23

**Template:** Marketing kit email (to be drafted)
**Merge fields:** {{First Name}}, {{Film Title}}, {{Venue Name}}

**What to do:**
1. Confirm marketing materials are in the shared Drive folder (poster PDF, social graphics, event description)
2. Draft email linking to the materials + social media copy suggestions
3. Point hosts to the host guide for additional marketing resources
4. Send test, then send to warm host list

**Decision rights:** You own the send. If materials aren't ready, delay and notify Garen.

---

### 3. Screening packet shipped — ~Apr 1

This overlaps with Stream 2, Step 2. The screening packet email IS the Stream 2 deliverable send. Don't send two separate emails.

**Decision rights:** Same as Stream 2. If you're unsure about overlap, check with Garen.

---

### 4. One week out — ~Apr 15

**Template:** "One week out" email (to be drafted)
**Merge fields:** {{First Name}}, {{Film Title}}, {{Screening Date}}, {{Venue Name}}

**What to do:**
1. Draft email: final logistics, slides/trailers link, emergency day-of contacts, a "you're ready" note
2. Include: tech support number, the host guide link, the presenter guide link
3. Send test, then send to warm host list
4. This is also when slides/trailers go out (Stream 2, Step 4) — combine into one email if possible

**Decision rights:** You own the send. Tone should be confident and reassuring, not anxious.

---

### 5. Eve of festival — ~Apr 21

**Template:** Eve of festival email (to be drafted)

**What to do:**
1. Keep this short. 3-4 sentences max.
2. Content: excitement, gratitude, emergency phone number, "we're here if you need anything"
3. No new information — everything they need was already sent
4. Send to warm host list

**Decision rights:** You own the send. Run the draft past Executive Director — this carries the organization's voice at a high-visibility moment.

---

### 6. Thank you + debrief survey — ~Apr 29

**Template:** Thank you + debrief email (to be drafted)
**Merge fields:** {{First Name}}, {{Venue Name}}, {{Survey Link}}

**What to do:**
1. Gratitude first. Thank them specifically for hosting.
2. Then link to the post-screening debrief survey (Stream 4, Form 3)
3. Keep the survey ask light — "5 minutes to help us improve for next year"
4. Send to warm host list

**Decision rights:** You own the send. Survey design should be finalized before this goes out (see Stream 4).

---

### 7. Follow up on survey non-respondents — ~May 5

**What to do:**
1. Export Form 3 responses. Identify hosts who haven't responded.
2. Send individual follow-up from hosts@ — short, friendly, one more ask
3. After this follow-up, don't push further

**Decision rights:** You own follow-ups. No third ask.

## Escalation

- **Film assignment changes after confirmation email** → Garen drafts an individual correction email. Don't send a mass update — only the affected host.
- **Marketing materials delayed** → Delay the send. Notify Garen. Don't promise dates you can't control.
- **Host asks about something outside the playbook** → Flag to Garen in Google Chat.
- **Eve of festival draft needs approval** → Route to Executive Director.

---

## Anchor questions

1. Did any milestone email generate unexpected questions that revealed a gap in our materials?
2. Was the timeline realistic, or did dates shift significantly?
3. Which milestone email got the most engagement (opens, replies)? That tells you what hosts care about most.
