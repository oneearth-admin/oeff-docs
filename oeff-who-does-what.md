## Ana · Year 15

  - Vision, budget, partnerships
  - Face and voice of the festival
  - The relationships that hold everything together
  - Batch host comms route through **Kim** directly from Ana
- **Defines:**
  - Scope, contracts, festival-week logistics, hiring
  - Anything that commits the organization
- **Greenlights:**
  - Public-facing comms, press
  - Sponsor commitments

- **Routing:**
  - Systems and process route through **Garen**
