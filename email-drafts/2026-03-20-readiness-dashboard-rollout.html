Full guide: OEFF 2026 — Host Helpers (and Readiness Dashboard)

The quick version: everything from the old tracker spreadsheet now lives in Airtable across linked tables (Venues, Events, Films, Contacts). The doc has a table showing where each piece of information ended up.

Three things you'll use day to day:

  1. Host Helper pages — each venue has its own link that shows the host their screening details, logistics, and registration count. Here's an example (Uncommon Ground).
  2. Host submission form — where hosts tell us about parking, WiFi, AV. View form.
  3. Readiness Dashboard — a color-coded view showing which venues are ready for link delivery. Open Airtable → Venues → "Readiness Dashboard" view.

Right now 21 venues are Green (ready), 2 are Yellow (need film or address), and the remaining prospects are hidden. The status updates automatically as data gets filled in.
