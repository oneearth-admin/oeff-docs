## Per-Webinar Content Blocks

### Mar 2 — Programming
**Agenda Bullets:**
- Your film selection and what makes it resonate with your community
- Per-film breakout calls — what to expect and how to prepare
- Discussion guide resources and audience engagement ideas

**Prep Notes:**
If you haven't finalized your film selection, please do so before Monday — email joshua@oneearthcollective.org.

### Mar 16 — Marketing/Promotion
**Agenda Bullets:**
- Promotional materials and how to customize them for your venue
- Social media toolkit and sample language
- Cross-promotion with other host venues

**Prep Notes:**
Think about your audience — who are you trying to reach, and what channels work best for your community?

### Mar 23 — Volunteer Recruitment & Training
**Agenda Bullets:**
- How many volunteers you'll need and what roles look like
- Recruitment tips that have worked for past hosts
- Training resources and what we'll cover in April sessions

**Prep Notes:**
Start thinking about who in your network might want to volunteer. Even 2-3 people makes a big difference.

### Mar 30 — AV/Tech Verification
**Agenda Bullets:**
- Walking through your screening setup — projector, sound, screen
- Testing your film file and troubleshooting common issues
- Day-of tech support and who to call if something goes wrong

**Prep Notes:**
If you can, test your projector or screen setup before Monday. Knowing your equipment helps us help you.
