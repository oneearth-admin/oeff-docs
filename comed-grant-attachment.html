oneearthfilmfest.org

Community-hosted model: OEFF does not operate a central theater. Each screening is hosted by a local organization — library, church, cultural center, school — that applies to participate. This embeds environmental film programming directly into neighborhoods, many with limited access to cultural events. All screenings are free or low-cost.
