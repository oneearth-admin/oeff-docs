Cards with a green border mark the longest-tenured team members — Ana (15 years), Erin (9), and Garen (7). They hold the institutional memory and keep the frame. Not managers, not gatekeepers. When a choice affects how the whole team works, it runs through them before it reaches the wider group.

Open Questions

What we still need to name

These are honest gaps, not oversights. Naming them is the first step toward resolving them.

  • Josh's scope
    Digital comms and day-of support is clear. What decisions can Josh make without checking with Ana? Where does WEJA sit? What's his greenlight boundary?
  • Michael's boundaries
    Fundraising + Launch Party + Opening Night is clear. "Event planning" without specifying which events creates confusion. How do we name that boundary?
  • When someone sees a gap
    The protocol should be: flag it, the right person picks it up, it goes out in the right voice. This needs to be shared with the team so everyone follows it.

These are on the agenda for the core stewards to resolve this week. Once named, the cards above get updated and this list gets shorter.
