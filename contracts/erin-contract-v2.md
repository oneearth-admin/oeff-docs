## Coordination

Day-to-day coordination between the Contractor and the Technical Coordinator is governed by a workflow document maintained separately from this agreement.

## Scope Boundaries

### Included

All work described in Areas 1 and 2, including coordination with other OEFF contractors and the Technical Coordinator.

### Not included

- Slide deck production and assembly (the Contractor may consult on brand consistency for the deck template in coordination with Marketing)
- AV system operation and technical setup (covered by day-of technical team)
- Technical specs (file formats, QC standards, equipment requirements)
- Process design or workflow architecture
- Contractor scoping or SOW creation
- Escalation decisions beyond documented triggers
- Equipment budgeting or vendor selection

### Scope change process

If new work arises beyond this scope, the Contractor will flag it before beginning. If it can be absorbed within the budget cap, a brief acknowledgment from the Executive Director via text, email, or verbal confirmation is sufficient. If it requires additional budget, both parties will discuss before work begins.

## Compensation & Invoicing

### Rate

$45 per hour, all work types. No distinction between creative and operations work.

### Budget cap

$5,490 total for the contract term. When 75% of the cap (~$4,118) has been invoiced, both parties will check in to review remaining scope and priorities.

### Invoicing

Monthly invoices submitted to finance@oneearthcollective.org with brief descriptions of work completed, submitted by the 5th of the following month. Payment within 30 days of invoice receipt.

All creative deliverables include one round of revision. This applies to recap videos, the welcome video, trailer reel, and sponsor acknowledgment content.
