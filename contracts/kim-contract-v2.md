## Festival Week On-Site Support (April 22-27) — Proposed

### Role

The Contractor provides on-site coordination support for host venue screenings during festival week, serving as a point of contact for hosts on the ground. During periods with simultaneous screenings, the Contractor prioritizes flagship events. Community screenings are supported by OEFF representatives and interns.

### Responsibilities

**Run of show coordination.** Communicate final ROS to hosts in advance. Walk hosts through the flow on arrival. Ensure each screening starts and ends as planned.

**Host check-ins.** Afternoon arrival at assigned venue(s). Confirm host readiness, resolve last-minute questions, and ensure hosts have what they need before doors open. In-person at flagship events.

**Program team relay.** Pick up and relay program team decisions to hosts in real time — schedule changes, speaker updates, audience flow adjustments.

**Intern coordination.** Serve as first line of contact for interns assigned to screenings. Delegate tasks, answer questions, route issues to the appropriate team lead.

**People wrangling.** Interface with venue tech teams, manage audience flow at the door, and handle the human logistics that keep a screening running smoothly.

**Escalation.** Route technical issues to the Technical Coordinator (remote) or the on-site AV team. Route programming issues to the Executive Director or Program Director.

### Pre-fest-week prep (included)

- Attend Monday host meetings in the weeks leading up to the festival
- Review final ROS documents for assigned venues
- Coordinate with the Technical Coordinator on venue-specific technical requirements
- Confirm intern assignments and brief interns on their roles

### Schedule

All 6 festival days: April 22 (Opening Night) through April 27 (Dominican reprise). Daily window: afternoon through beginning of screening (~4-6 hours/day depending on venue and event complexity). Heavier days are Thu-Sat when multiple host sites are active simultaneously.
