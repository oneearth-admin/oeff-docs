Risk Summary

Single points of failure: Data Pipeline, Domain Docs, and V7 Health Monitor have no backup owner. These are the highest-risk systems if Garen became unavailable. The pipeline and health monitor are also the most technically complex to hand off (Python scripts, API knowledge, FFmpeg expertise).
Strongest handoff candidates: Host Communications (YAMM) and Support Inbox are the most ready for delegation. Kim is actively onboarding into both. The comms plan provides a complete calendar, and the support docs provide response templates. These could be fully transferred by mid-March with supervised practice.
