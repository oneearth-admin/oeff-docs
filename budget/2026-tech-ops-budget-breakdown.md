## Section 6: Cost Savings vs Prior Years

This year's model is fundamentally different from 2024-2025. The smaller crew reflects intentional scope reduction, not a gap.

| Prior-Year Cost | 2026 Status | Estimated Savings |
|-----------------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Livestreaming / hybrid production** | Eliminated. No hybrid events except Dominican (self-supported). | ~$3,000-$5,000 |
| **OEFF-supplied AV equipment** (projectors, screens, PA) | Eliminated. All venues either have own tech (T1) or need only a laptop (T2). | ~$2,000-$3,500 |
| **Expanded AV crew** (2-4 person team) | Replaced by intern support + per-event content coordinators. Digital natives; laptop playback is within capability. | ~$2,000-$4,000 |
| **Dedicated on-site technical director** | Replaced by remote coordination (run-of-show docs, playback instructions, troubleshooting support). | ~$1,500-$2,500 |
| **Equipment transport & logistics** | Eliminated. No equipment to transport. | ~$500-$1,000 |

**Estimated total savings vs prior-year model: ~$9,000-$16,000**

These savings are what make the new scope possible — program design support, operations coordination, pre-fest comms infrastructure — higher-value work that builds organizational capacity instead of repeating the same event-day labor each year.
