Host Comms Dependency Map

OEFF 2026 — Garen Hudson — Feb 16, 2026

Purpose: Each communication stream has dependencies that must be satisfied before a send can happen. This map makes those dependencies explicit so that handoff to Kim (or any future operator) includes the full chain — not just "send this email" but "this email requires these prerequisites, these approvals, and this fallback plan."
Stream 1: Webinar Reminders & Recording Follow-ups
2-day-before reminders + 1-day-after recording follow-ups for 5 webinars + 2 office hours
Data Source Google Sheets Webinar Comms sheet — columns: First Name, Email, Webinar Date, Topic, Recording Link, Status
Approval Owner Garen (autonomous per decision-rights matrix — follows approved comms plan calendar)
Send Owner Garen (transitioning to Kim in Phase 2). Manual YAMM trigger from hosts@ Gmail.
Rollback Owner Garen. If wrong link or incorrect date sent: send correction email within 2 hours. YAMM has no recall feature.
Prerequisites
  • Webinar date/time confirmed on OEFF calendar
  • Webinar topic finalized (for subject line merge field)
  • Reminder template drafted and reviewed
  • For follow-ups: recording uploaded and link obtained
  • For follow-ups: 2-3 sentence recap written (Claude-assisted)
  • Host guide updated with recording link before follow-up send
Downstream
  • If reminder fails to send: lower webinar attendance, hosts unprepared
  • If follow-up delayed: hosts lose trust in communication reliability
  • If host guide not updated before follow-up: broken link in email
Stream 2: Deliverables Shipping (OEFF to Hosts)
Screening packets, promo materials, slides/trailers, presenter guide, t-shirts
Data Source Google Sheets Warm host sheet — columns: Film Title, Screening Date, Venue Tier, Film Delivered, Promo Sent, Slides Sent, T-shirt Size/Shipped
Approval Owner Garen for technical deliverables (film files, slides). Ana for promo materials and any new messaging.
Send Owner Garen. Three YAMM templates: screening packet (3 wks out), slides/trailers (1 wk out), final check-in (day before).
Rollback Owner Garen. If wrong film sent to wrong venue: immediately email correct file + apology. If download link expires: regenerate and resend individually.
Prerequisites
  • Film-to-venue matching complete (Ana decision)
  • Film files QC'd, captioned, and uploaded to secure download
  • Per-host download links generated (password-protected)
  • Promo materials finalized by Cesar
  • Google Slides deck built with per-venue data
  • Presenter guide (tonight-runsheet.html) updated with 2026 data
  • T-shirt sizes collected via Form 1 before shipping
Downstream
  • If film files not delivered 3 weeks out: hosts cannot test playback
  • If slides delayed: venues run screenings without pre-show content
  • If presenter guide late: hosts improvise run-of-show (quality risk)
  • If t-shirt sizes not collected: wrong sizes shipped or no shirts
Stream 3: Milestone Communications
Broader updates at key moments: film confirmation, marketing kit, one-week-out, thank-you
Data Source Google Sheets Warm host sheet (same as Stream 2). Milestone dates from comms plan calendar.
Approval Owner Ana for film confirmation messaging and festival-eve communications. Garen for logistical milestone sends per approved calendar.
Send Owner Garen (or Kim for routine milestones post-handoff). YAMM from hosts@ Gmail.
Rollback Owner Garen for logistics errors. Ana for messaging/relationship issues. Festival-eve email corrections require immediate response.
Prerequisites
  • "Film confirmed" email: film matching complete + synopsis/trailer available
  • "Marketing kit" email: Cesar's assets finalized and uploaded
  • "Screening packet" email: all Stream 2 prerequisites met
  • "One week out" email: slides done, presenter guide done, emergency contacts confirmed
  • "Eve of festival" email: emergency phone number confirmed, all logistics locked
  • "Thank you" email: debrief survey (Form 3) built and tested
Downstream
  • If "film confirmed" delayed: hosts cannot promote their screening
  • If "marketing kit" late: hosts have less promotion runway
  • If "eve of festival" missed: hosts feel unsupported going into event
  • If "thank you" delayed: post-festival goodwill window closes
Stream 4: Data Collection from Hosts (Surveys)
Three Google Forms: pre-event readiness, day-of confirmation, post-screening debrief
Data Source Google Forms Three forms, responses flow into Google Sheets. Sent via YAMM with form links.
Approval Owner Ana approves form questions before build. Garen approves form structure and data flow.
Send Owner Garen (Kim can send once trained). Form 1: 3 weeks before. Form 2: 2 days before. Form 3: 2 days after.
Rollback Owner Garen for form errors (wrong questions, broken links). If form link broken: resend with correct link + apology.
Prerequisites
  • Form 1 (readiness): Google Form built, tested, response sheet linked
  • Form 1: screening dates confirmed (so "confirm your date" question makes sense)
  • Form 2 (day-of): Form built, extremely short (3 questions max)
  • Form 2: film files already delivered (so "film plays correctly?" question is valid)
  • Form 3 (debrief): Form built with open-text fields for qualitative capture
  • Form 3: sent 2 days after screening, not during festival week (no mass emails that week)
Downstream
  • If Form 1 responses not monitored: t-shirt sizes missing, AV issues undetected
  • If Form 2 reveals issues: day-of team must triage immediately (escalate to Garen)
  • If Form 3 not sent: no post-festival data for retrospective or 2027 planning
  • Form 3 responses feed Claude analysis for lessons-learned doc
Stream 5: Cool Host Conversion
Separate pipeline for interested-but-uncommitted venues. Goal: convert to warm host list.
Data Source Google Sheets Cool host sheet — columns: Name, Email, Venue, Status (Contacted / Form Sent / Responded / Converted)
Approval Owner Ana for initial outreach messaging and film list availability. Garen for send timing and follow-up cadence.
Send Owner Garen (Kim can manage pipeline post-handoff). YAMM for initial send; individual follow-ups as needed.
Rollback Owner Garen for logistics. Ana if a cool host has a pre-existing relationship that requires sensitive handling.
Prerequisites
  • Film list PDF finalized and available for linking
  • Film selection Google Form built (name, venue, top choices, date preference)
  • Cool host sheet populated with all interested-but-uncommitted contacts
  • Conversion email template drafted and approved by Ana
  • Host guide live and linkable (already done)
Downstream
  • If conversion email delayed: fewer venues for 2026 festival
  • When cool host converts: move to warm host sheet, enters all other streams
  • If film list not ready: cool hosts can't select, conversion stalls
  • Unconverted cool hosts remain in pipeline for 2027 outreach

Cross-Stream Dependencies

Critical chain: Stream 5 (cool host conversion) feeds into Stream 2 (deliverables) and Stream 3 (milestones). A late conversion means compressed timelines for film delivery and screening prep. Any cool host converting after March 15 will receive an accelerated onboarding sequence.
Shared infrastructure: All five streams depend on the warm host Google Sheet being accurate and current. This sheet is the single point of truth for merge fields. If the sheet has stale data (wrong email, wrong film title, wrong date), every stream produces incorrect output. Sheet hygiene is not a nice-to-have — it is load-bearing infrastructure.

Failure cascade example

If film-to-venue matching (Ana decision) is delayed past March 9:

  • Stream 3 "film confirmed" email cannot send (no film to confirm)
  • Stream 2 screening packet cannot be assembled (no film assigned)
  • Stream 4 Form 1 "confirm your screening date" question becomes premature
  • Stream 1 webinar content may reference films hosts don't have yet

Mitigation: Film matching is the highest-priority Ana decision for March. Flag in weekly 1:1 starting Feb 24.
